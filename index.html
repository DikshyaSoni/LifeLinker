AI Chatbot Helpline

Get 24/7 assistance for FAQs, eligibility checks, and donation guidance through our intelligent AI chatbot. Simply click the chat icon in the bottom right corner to get instant help.

  • Instant answers to frequently asked questions
  • Eligibility verification for blood donation
  • Step-by-step donation process guidance

Health & Recovery Tips

Receive personalized in-app guidance for donors before and after donation, including hydration tips, rest recommendations, and nutrition advice to ensure a smooth donation experience.

  • Pre-donation preparation checklist
  • Post-donation recovery guidelines
  • Nutrition recommendations for healthy donors
  • Hydration and rest advice

Donor Medical History

Maintain a comprehensive record of your donation history, last donation date, and eligibility status. This helps both donors and medical professionals track donation schedules and ensure safety.

  • Complete donation history tracking
  • Last donation date reminders
  • Eligibility status updates
  • Health screening records

About Our Donors

Our community of blood donors is the heart of Life-Linkers. These selfless individuals have committed to saving lives through regular blood donation. Each donor plays a crucial role in our mission to ensure that no patient goes without the blood they need in times of emergency or medical treatment.

With over 40 registered donors across multiple cities, our network continues to grow. Our donors come from diverse backgrounds but share a common goal - to make a difference in their communities by giving the gift of life.
